Hummus

Description

This is a recipe for Lebanese-style hummus. Adapted from Maha's Lebanese Cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 Cup Garbanzo beans
  • 1/3 Cup Tahini sauce
  • 1/4 Cup Lemon juice
  • 3 Tbsp Olive oil
  • 2/3 Cup water (add slowly)
  • .5 Tsp Salt or to taste

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together in food processor and enjoy!
