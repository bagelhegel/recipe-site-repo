Foul (Egyptian Fava Beans)

Description

While this is considered Egypt's national dish, it is enjoyed throughout the middle east! Adapted from The Mediterranean Dish. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Fava beans (canned or dry)
  • Seasoning & lemon garlic sauce with chili peppers
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 Tbsp Olive oil
  • Parsley
  • Diced tomatoes

Steps

  1. Heat, mash and season fava beans
  2. Add spicy lemon-garlic sauce and extra virgin olive oil
  3. Add fresh toppings
